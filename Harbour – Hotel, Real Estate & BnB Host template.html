  • What types of rooms are available at Harbour?

    Harbour offers a selection of luxurious rooms and suites, each designed to provide the ultimate comfort. Choose from cliffside rooms with panoramic coastal views, spacious suites with private terraces, or secluded estate rooms for added privacy. Every room features premium furnishings and modern amenities.

  • What activities can I enjoy at Harbour?

    Harbour is a haven for adventure and relaxation. Enjoy championship golf, scenic hiking trails across 100 acres of pristine land, a heated outdoor pool, and a state-of-the-art gym. For those seeking relaxation, our world-class spa offers a range of treatments to rejuvenate your body and mind.

  • Are dining options available at Harbour?

    Yes, Harbour boasts an exquisite dining experience with seasonal menus crafted from locally sourced ingredients. Enjoy your meals in our fine dining restaurant overlooking the coastline or savour casual bites by the pool.

  • Is Harbour family-friendly?

    Absolutely. Harbour welcomes guests of all ages. Families can enjoy spacious accommodations, outdoor activities, and tailored spa treatments, ensuring a memorable experience for every guest.
